Parksville Tours

We offer four different routes around parksville. See info for each of them below. If you have any questions after this, please don't hesitate to call us or send us an email.

Town Route

The town route is for anyone, this route includes the best places in the town of parksville. And the most common and central parks. As you can see below, the estimated time is 45 minutes. The full ride including sightseeing and picking up trash takes about 1 hour and 15 minutes.

Book Now

Forest Route

The forest route takes about 3 hours and you will see the beautiful forests of parksville. Wildlife sights is guaranteed with this route, and you will not be dissapointed. this route is a little bit advanced and bouncy, but don't worry, we know the best routes to take to avoid such terrain.

Book Now

Mountain Route

The mountain route is a bit more advanced and takes about 5 hours and 30 minutes to complete. We do offer car drives to the mountain-foot where you meet up with the rest of the group and go from there. this trip includes some amazing views, high chance of seeing animals and it's a pretty good workout.

Book Now

Nomad Route

The nomad route is for the hardcore bikers out there. this route takes about 8 hours and includes town sightseeing, forest sightseeing, mountain sightseeing and coastline sightseeing. This is the best route for sure, but it is physically challanging and require some conditioning.

Book Now
