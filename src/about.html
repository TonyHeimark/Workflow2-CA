About Us

We're a team consisting of 11 passionate cyclists. 9 years ago we were going on a trip to one of our favorite places to visit by bike. When we arrived, what met us was hundreds of tourist and piles of trash.


It ruined the experience for us, and we came to think about the tourists aswell. They all came here because they have seen pictures and videos of this beautiful place. This did not look like the picture.


So we came up with this idea, what if we could go on cycling trips together, meet other amazing people and show them unbeliveable places around Parksville, While picking up trash.

We spent some time trying to gather equipment, which was expensive. One day we got approached by the leaders of the organisation Green Canada. They desided to fund us 100% with some of the donations the organisation recieved.


We were over the moon with happiness. We could now bring a bunch of people with us on these amazing trips and at the same time, save a little bit of nature on the way.


We change our routes every 6 months, to make sure that we pick up trash from different places while still visiting the awesome locations.
