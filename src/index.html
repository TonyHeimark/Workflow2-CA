Parksville
Free Guided Cycling Tours

Re-Cycle is all about showing the beautiful nature of Parksville to tourists and anyone that would like to explore it. While at the same time keeping it free from trash and plastic.


We offer free guided tours and we provide all the equipment you need. What do we want in return? It's simple, all we want is for you to help us pick up trash and plastic on the way.

Our tours explore parks, mountains, the town itself and the coastline. Depending on your choice of route. You will explore lots of landmarks and wildlife. Get new memories and experiences!

We take care of
our environment

Sadly there's a lot of trash out there and it's ruining our nature and potentially hurting animals. This is our solution as cyclists, we get to show people our beautiful town, do good deeds and do what we love at the same time!


Last year we picked up 1780 lbs of trash. Join us and everyone else to beat that record.
